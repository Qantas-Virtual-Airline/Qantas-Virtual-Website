Military Ratings

M0 → M4 Progression

M0
No Military Rating
DEFAULT
Standard civilian member. No military access.
M1
Military Pilot Licence
ENTRY
Complete Military Induction + 10 military hours. Transport missions, basic exercises.
Unlocks: C-130 transport, basic patrol, exercises
M2
Military Instrument Rating
INTERMEDIATE
M1 + IFR military procedures + 25 total military hours. Night and all-weather operations.
Unlocks: Night ops, maritime patrol, all-weather
M3
Military Multi-Engine Rating
ADVANCED
M2 + multi-engine military ops + 50 total military hours. Heavy transport and tanker.
Unlocks: C-17 Globemaster, KC-30A tanker ops
M4
Military Mission Ready Pilot
ELITE
M3 + 100 military hours + staff endorsement. Mission lead authority.
Unlocks: Special Ops, mission lead, classified exercises
