Mandatory Tools

🗺️
SIMBRIEF
simbrief.com · FLIGHT PLANNING

Generate a full Operational Flight Plan before every departure. Provides routing, fuel, weather, NOTAMs and performance data. Load directly into your FMS.

⚠
Mandatory. Flights without a SimBrief OFP will NOT count toward hours or rank.

📋
FLIGHT LOGGER
QFV Discord Bot · FLIGHT LOGGING

Log every completed flight immediately after landing using the Flight Logger bot in the QFV Discord. Required fields: Flight Number · Aircraft · Departure · Arrival · Block Time.

⚠
Mandatory. Flights not logged via Flight Logger will NOT count. No manual submissions.
