Mandatory Tools

Every QFV flight requires both tools. No exceptions. Flights without SimBrief planning or Flight Logger submissions will not count toward your hours or rank.

🗺️
SimBrief
simbrief.com
Generate a full Operational Flight Plan before every departure. SimBrief provides routing, fuel calculations, weather, NOTAMs, weight & balance, and performance data. Load your OFP directly into your simulator FMS.
⚠ Flights without a SimBrief OFP will NOT count toward hours or rank progression.
📋
Flight Logger
QFV Discord Bot
The official QFV flight logging bot, available in our Discord server. Log every completed flight immediately after landing. Required fields: Flight Number · Aircraft Type · Departure · Arrival · Block Time.
⚠ Flights not logged via Flight Logger will NOT count. No manual submissions accepted.
