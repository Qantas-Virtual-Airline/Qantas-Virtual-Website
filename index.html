Mandatory Tools

🗺️
SIMBRIEF
simbrief.com — FLIGHT PLANNING

Generate a full Operational Flight Plan before every departure. SimBrief provides routing, fuel calculations, NOTAM awareness, weather, and weight & balance. Load directly into your FMS via SimBrief integration.

⚠
Mandatory. Flights without a SimBrief OFP will NOT count toward hours or rank.

📋
FLIGHT LOGGER
QFV Discord Bot — FLIGHT LOGGING

Log every completed flight immediately after landing using the Flight Logger bot in the QFV Discord. Required fields: Flight Number · Aircraft · Departure ICAO · Arrival ICAO · Block Time. Submit within 48 hours.

⚠
Mandatory. Flights not logged via Flight Logger will NOT count. No manual submissions.
