PRIVACY & GDPR

Last updated: March 2025 · Version 1.0

✓
By using the QFV website and Discord, you consent to the use of your data as described. You may withdraw at any time via the Support page.
1. WHO WE ARE

Qantas Virtual (QFV) is an independent virtual airline community for flight simulation. We have no legal affiliation with Qantas Airways Limited, VATSIM, or any real-world aviation entity.

2. DATA WE COLLECT

VATSIM CID (voluntary) · Display name · Flight log data · Browser session state · Cookie consent preference. We do not collect email addresses, passwords, or payment data.

3. HOW WE USE IT

To track flight hours, maintain the public roster, comply with VATSIM VAP audit requirements, and operate QFV features. We do not sell or share your data with any third party.

4. STORAGE

Hosted on GitHub Pages. Login state is stored in browser sessionStorage only — cleared when you close your tab. Flight logs stored in localStorage — browser only. Flight Logger bot data is governed by Discord's privacy policy.

5. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, delete, port, or object to processing of your data. Contact us via the Support page. Requests processed within 30 days.

6. COOKIES

Essential session cookies only. No tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies. Declining cookies does not affect site access.

7. ANTI-PIRACY POLICY

All software used by QFV members must be properly licensed. Pirated software is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate removal. VATSIM will be notified of violations within 30 days.

8. CONTACT

Privacy enquiries via the Support page. Staff contact details are not published publicly.
