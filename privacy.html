By using the QFV website and Discord server, you consent to the collection and use of your data as described in this policy. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us via the Support page.
1. WHO WE ARE

Qantas Virtual (QFV) is an independent virtual airline community for flight simulation on the VATSIM network. We are not affiliated with Qantas Airways Limited, VATSIM, or any real-world aviation organisation. We operate as an independent online community.

2. DATA WE COLLECT

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate QFV. This includes:

•
VATSIM CID: Your VATSIM member identifier, provided voluntarily when joining QFV
•
Display name / callsign: Chosen by you for use within QFV
•
Flight log data: Flight numbers, routes, aircraft types, and block times logged via Flight Logger
•
Session data: Browser session storage for login state — cleared when you close your browser
•
Cookie consent: Whether you have accepted or declined our cookie notice
3. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

Your data is used solely to: track flight hours and rank progression, maintain the public pilot roster, comply with VATSIM VAP audit requirements, and operate QFV community features. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party, except where required by VATSIM VAP audit processes (flight log data only).

4. DATA STORAGE

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. No personal data is stored on our web server — login state is stored only in your own browser's session storage and cleared when you close your tab. Flight log data is stored within the QFV Discord server via the Flight Logger bot, governed by Discord's own privacy policy.

5. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR)

6. COOKIES

We use only essential functional cookies (session storage) to maintain your login state during a browsing session. No tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies are used. You may decline cookies via the consent banner — this will not affect your ability to access the site, only your login session.

7. ANTI-PIRACY POLICY

In accordance with VATSIM VAP requirements: all software used by QFV members must be properly licensed. The use of pirated software is strictly prohibited. Any member found using pirated software will be immediately removed. QFV will notify VATSIM of any such violations within 30 days as required.

8. CONTACT

For any privacy-related enquiries, data requests, or concerns, please use our Support page. We do not publish direct email addresses publicly.
