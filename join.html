What You Need

Microsoft Flight Simulator 2020/2024, X-Plane 12, or Prepar3D V5+. You'll also need a VATSIM pilot client: vPilot (MSFS/P3D) or xPilot (XP12).

Application Steps

How to Join

1
Join the QFV Discord

Click the button below. Read #rules carefully and react with ✅ to unlock member access. Do not skip this — unverified members cannot apply.

2
Post Your Application

In #pilot-applications, paste this template:

Name/Callsign: [your name]
VATSIM CID: [your CID]
Simulator: [MSFS / XP12 / P3D]
Experience: [Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced]
Why QFV?:

3
Staff Review (48hrs)

A staff member will review your application within 48 hours. You may receive follow-up questions via DM. Applications are approved unless there's a specific reason to decline.

4
Get Your Pilot ID

Once accepted, you receive your Pilot ID (e.g. QFV005), starting rank, and access to all pilot channels. You're in!

5
Complete Onboarding & Fly

Read POL-001, POL-002, and your aircraft SOP. Generate your first SimBrief OFP, file on VATSIM, fly your first domestic route, and log it with Flight Logger. You're officially a QFV pilot.
