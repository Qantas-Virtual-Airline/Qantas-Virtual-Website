About Qantas Virtual

Qantas Virtual (QFV) is an independent virtual airline operating on the VATSIM network, modelled on the real-world Qantas Airways. We are a VATSIM Virtual Airline Affiliate (VAA) and are actively working toward Virtual Airline Partner (VAP) status.

Founded in 2025, QFV started with a small group of dedicated sim pilots who wanted a professional, structured virtual airline experience on VATSIM. Today we operate three divisions: Civilian, Military, and ATC Training.

Our training programme aligns with official VATSIM ratings (P0–P6, M1–M4, S1–C3) and prepares pilots for ATO examinations. We use SimBrief for every flight and Flight Logger to track all hours.
