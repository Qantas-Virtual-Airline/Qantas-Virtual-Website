Departure

Departure Procedure

1
Pre-flight & SimBrief OFP
Generate your Operational Flight Plan at simbrief.com using this aircraft's ICAO code. Review fuel, routing, alternates, and performance data. Load the OFP into your FMS via SimBrief integration.
2
ATIS & Clearance
Obtain ATIS on the published frequency. Contact Clearance Delivery (or Ground if no DEL) for your IFR clearance. Confirm SID, squawk, initial altitude, and departure frequency. Readback clearance in full.
3
Engine Start & Pushback
Complete pre-start checks per aircraft checklist. Request pushback from Ground if required. Start engines per normal start procedure. Monitor engine parameters throughout.
4
Taxi & Line-Up
After receiving taxi clearance, follow the assigned routing to the runway. Hold short as instructed. Confirm runway assignment matches IFR clearance before entering.
5
Departure
On Tower clearance, advance thrust smoothly to flex/TOGA thrust. Follow SID routing precisely. Engage autopilot when above minimum engagement altitude. Check in with Departure on handoff.
Arrival

Arrival & Approach

1
Descent Planning
Begin top-of-descent planning 200–250nm from destination. Use FMS PROG page for descent profile. Obtain ATIS well in advance. Brief approach plate and minima.
2
STAR & Approach
Follow assigned STAR routing. Manage speed per ATC instructions and STAR constraints. Configure aircraft per approach profile. Brief go-around procedure.
3
Final Approach
Establish on final at or below glideslope. Complete landing checklist. Stabilised approach criteria: on speed, on profile, configured, by 1,000ft AAL. If not stabilised — go around.
4
Landing & Rollout
Maintain centreline throughout flare. Apply reverse thrust and autobrake/manual braking as required. Clear the runway at the first available exit.
5
Post-Landing & Log
Complete after-landing checklist. Taxi to gate per Ground instructions. Shutdown per checklist. Log your flight via the Pilot Dashboard within 48 hours.
