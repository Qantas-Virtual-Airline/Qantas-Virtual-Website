COMMON QUESTIONS
How do I join QFV?

Join our Discord, read the rules, and post your application in #pilot-applications. See the Join page for full instructions.

My Flight Logger hours are wrong.

Contact the Vice President with your Flight Logger log ID, flight details, and what you believe is incorrect. Include screenshots if possible.

How do I apply for the Military Division?

You need P2 rating and 10+ QFV hours. Post in the military applications channel on Discord or email the VP. See the Military page.

How do I appeal a disciplinary decision?

Email the President directly with your appeal. Appeals must be submitted within 7 days of the decision. Decisions made after review are final.

I want to become a Staff member or Director.

Watch for role openings in #role-openings on Discord. You must be Verified with at least 25 QFV hours. Contact the VP to express interest.

How do I get my VATSIM rating?

QFV prepares you for VATSIM ratings. Official pilot ratings are issued through VATSIM ATOs. ATC ratings through VATPAC. See the Ranks page for more.
