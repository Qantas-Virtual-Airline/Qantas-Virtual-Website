Quick Help

Common Questions

How do I join QFV?

See the Join page for full instructions. Apply via Discord after reading the rules.

My Flight Logger hours are wrong.

Submit a support request selecting "Hours / Rank Dispute". Include your log entry ID and flight details.

How do I apply for the Military Division?

You need P2 rating and 10+ QFV hours. Select "Military Division Application" in the form above.

How do I appeal a decision?

Select "Appeal a Decision" above. Appeals must be within 7 days of the decision.

GDPR data request

Select "GDPR / Data Request". See our Privacy Policy for your rights.

I want to become staff.

Watch #role-openings in Discord. You need Verified status and 25+ QFV hours minimum.
