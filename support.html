Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

How do I join QFV?
+

Fill in the application form. Staff review within 48 hours. Have VATSIM, SimBrief, and a simulator ready before applying.

My flight hours are wrong or missing.
+

Submit a ticket selecting "Hours / Rank Dispute". Include your Pilot ID, the flight date, flight number, and block time. SimBrief OFP reference helps if you have it.

How do I apply for the Military Division?
+

P2 (Instrument Rating) + 10 logged QFV civilian hours required. Submit a "Military Division Application" ticket with your Pilot ID, current rating, and total hours.

How do I appeal a decision?
+

Submit an "Appeal a Decision" ticket within 7 days of the decision. Include the original decision details and grounds for appeal. The President reviews all appeals personally.

SimBrief isn't loading into the flight log form.
+

Ensure your SimBrief username is saved in your Dashboard profile. OFPs expire after 24h — generate a fresh one and retry. If still broken, log manually and submit a Technical ticket.

GDPR — how do I request or delete my data?
+

All QFV site data is in your own browser localStorage — clear it via browser DevTools → Application → Local Storage. For Discord/server-side data, submit a "GDPR / Data Request" ticket. See our Privacy Policy.
