STRUCTURE

QFV uses the official VATSIM rating system for all pilot, military and ATC ratings. Hours are tracked exclusively via the Flight Logger bot.

📌
Important — VAP Status: QFV is currently a VATSIM Virtual Airline Affiliate (VAA). We do not issue official VATSIM pilot or ATC ratings at this time. All VATSIM ratings (PPL, IR, ATPL etc.) must be obtained through an official VATSIM Authorised Training Organisation (ATO). Our training program prepares you for those certifications. When QFV achieves VAP status, we will apply to become an ATO and issue ratings directly.
